My trip to Eswatini, visited Mlilwane Wildlife Sanctuary

Hi friends!! Welcome to my Travel blog page where I share with you my Eswatini trip

Eswatini was formerly known as Swaziland, I am sure you know it by the name Swaziland if you studied in my era the old unupdate Social Studies books. The country name changed to Eswatini in 2018. Interesting fact, the name was changed because I assume most people landed in Swaziland thinking it was Switzerland. Almost similar to the Austria and Australia situation. Mlilwane National Park is perfect for a more intimate encounter with nature as it has a hike scenic trail with a view of horse riding through rolling hills and cycling alongside zebras and antelopes in a serene, tourist-friendly setting.

Gallery: Fire Island

Explore the Adventure at Mlilwane Wildlife Sanctuary

Imagine Mlilwane Wildlife Sanctuary as a park that has hidden treasures to unravel. The park has a lot to be explored more than 350 species of birds, Parks of lions as well as elephant species not found in Kenya or East Africa.
If that hasn't sold you the idea to visit then well maybe one image from my visit should do the convincing. An extra tip invest in a cozy camp site for the best experience. Carry your camera too or the latest iphone 17!
